- Who are we? -

Ambrosia Raw

Our aim is to share with you our incredible, tasty and 100% RAW and healthy snacks, designed to help you satisfy your cravings without negatively impacting your health.

Ambrosia Raw snack jars are rich and creamy with a smooth taste that is delicious and absolutely heavenly - a natural indulgence, with all the goodness of a treat, into a convenient snack on the go.

Because it’s 100% RAW, each decadent snack jar retains it’s nutritional value, to ensure your insides stay happy.

Perfect for children, adults and everyone else looking for a natural and healthy alternative.

raw, vegan friendly, gluten and dairy free treats

Once you’ve tried just one mouthful of any of our snack jars, we’re sure you’ll agree - it tastes better naturally!
